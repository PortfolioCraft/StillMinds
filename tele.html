Teletherapy Sessions

Healthcare has changed, and so too has mental health care. Through the use of secure video-conferencing technology, mental health care providers and clients can still engage in teletherapy without being in the same physical location. Teletherapy can be helpful in increasing access to care by removing distance, transportation, or mobility barriers for clients. For clients who have great working relationships with providers, it offers continuity of care in the case of within-state relocation or travel. As we've seen during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, it has been a saving grace to offer safe support in a unprecedented time.  Though teletherapy has its value and is beneficial for many clients, it isn’t appropriate for everyone or every problem or concern. If you’re interested in seeing if teletherapy is right for you, let’s talk.
